Property finance calculators

See how much finance your client could have and how much it would cost them. We also have a Buy-to-Let Affordability calculator and a Rental Yield calculator.

How much finance can my client have?

Takes all applicants’ income and credit commitments into account and provides a maximum finance figure.

Go to calculator

How much will it cost them?

Provides a monthly payment amount based on term, finance amount, the type of agreement and the purpose of the finance.

Go to calculator

Buy-to-let Affordability

Covers Buy-to-Let, HMOs and MUFB's. Uses finance amount to determine minimum rent required, or estimated rental income to determine finance required.

Go to calculator

Rental Yield calculator

Our Rental Yield Calculator allows you to work out the gross and net rental yields on any single property or your entire portfolio. You can choose our quick calculator or expand the fields to add more detail as you require.
