Commercial Property Finance

Commercial Investment > Semi-Commercial > Owner-Occupied

We offer Commercial Property Finance for both purchase and re-finance (including capital raising) for Commercial Investment, Semi-Commercial and Owner-Occupied purposes from £500k up to £2m, for both UK residents/entities and UK Expat/International residents.

We understand that one transaction will never be the same as another, and for this reason, we assess each proposal on its own merits, offering a personal service and a flexible underwriting approach.

What we Offer

  • Commercial Investment, Semi-Commercial and Owner-Occupied finance
  • Purchase and Re-finance (including Capital raising)
  • Available to UK residents/entities and UK Expat/International residents
  • Flexible underwriting and competitive rates
  • Financing up to 70% of the property value or purchase price, whichever is the lower;
  • Financing amounts of between £500k and £2m
  • One main point of contact from Enquiry through to completion

Property types include

  • Office
  • Industrial
  • Warehousing
  • Retail
  • Retail with attached living accommodation
  • Purpose built student accommodation

Eligibility

  • Property must be located in England or Wales
  • Property valuation must be carried out by Gatehouse Bank approved surveyor
