Completions Officer

Based in Milton Keynes

Job title: Completions Officer

Location: Milton Keynes

Closing date: 31 May 2019

The purpose of this role is to ensure all home finance applications are progressed through to completion in an efficient and timely way. Liaising and communicating with various internal and external stakeholders throughout the journey to completion. The incumbent is required to ensure service levels are maintained whilst being mindful of the Credit Risk Governance frameworks in operation within the Bank, such as KYC and Fraud Awareness

About us

Gatehouse Bank is a subsidiary of Gatehouse Financial Group Limited, which is a holding company for Gatehouse Bank (based in London and Milton Keynes) and Gatehouse Capital (based in Kuwait). Gatehouse Bank is a Shariah-compliant bank offering savings products and finance for UK commercial and residential real estate, in addition to sourcing and advising on UK real estate investments with a focus on the build to rent sector.

Key responsibilities

• Liase with Banks’ Solicitors to progress case through to completion, ensuring Bank’s requirements are fulfilled.

• Work collaboratively with colleagues, brokers, applicants, solicitors to progress case in an efficient and timely way.

• Any other duties commensurate with the grade and level of responsibility of this post, for which the post holder has the necessary experience and/or training.

Required knowledge & skills

• Experience within a home purchase plan or other lending related process driven operational team.

• Service excellence delivery for customers

• Time management

• Fraud risk/compliance awareness

• Regulatory standards awareness – MCOB
