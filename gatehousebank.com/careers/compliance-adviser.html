Compliance Adviser

Based in Milton Keynes

Job title: Compliance Adviser

Location: Milton Keynes

Closing date: 31 May 2019

To assist the Compliance Officer MK to identify, assesses, mitigate and control through compliance monitoring. Particular focus will be on the operational aspects of the newly created residential Home Purchase Plans (HPP) business and unregulated mortgages.

To assist in undertaking compliance monitoring and reviews to verify provision of management information, on the bank’s adherence to the regulatory rules and the best practice that is discharged through the operational environment in BAU. Reporting and communicating issues as appropriate.

To assist in ensuring appropriate compliance oversight (adherence to MCOB and all other associated relevant regulatory rules), over the operational environment from origination to back end service provided to the Bank (under SYSC8).

About us

Gatehouse Bank is a subsidiary of Gatehouse Financial Group Limited, which is a holding company for Gatehouse Bank (based in London and Milton Keynes) and Gatehouse Capital (based in Kuwait). Gatehouse Bank is a Shariah-compliant bank offering savings products and finance for UK commercial and residential real estate, in addition to sourcing and advising on UK real estate investments with a focus on the build to rent sector.

Key Responsibilities

Role duties:

  • To support Mk Compliance Officer in discharging their regulatory compliance responsibilities across the business, more specifically as it relates to HPP and unregulated mortgages. Inclusive of end to end process controls.
  • To assist in ensuring the Bank remains fully compliant with all statutory and regulatory obligations through active management and compliance monitoring of all aspects of regulatory compliance.
  • To assist in undertake horizon scanning of new regulatory impacts on the Bank.
  • To develop knowledge of relevant regulatory handbooks (MCOB) and sourcebook, and industry best practice as is applicable to the business model of the Bank.
  • To assist the MK Compliance Officer in the provision of advice as it relates to or impacts the Banks operational environment.
  • To assist the MK Compliance officer in ensuring the Bank remains fully compliant in discharging its AML obligations and to identify any instances of financial crime, fraud, ABC, or conflict of interest.
  • To assist the MK Compliance Officer to undertake compliance monitoring of the Banks adherence to policies and procedures (from origination through to decision in principle and monitoring of back end outsourced servicing activities for the Bank) and also in the following:
    - Review and monitoring of policies and procedures.
    - Review of operational process in the Direct Finance team.
    - To advise the business on proposed activities and the regulatory impact from a financial crime perspective.
    - To review/assist, as required, with on-boarding and periodic files, compliance review sign-off and provision of monthly MI (including Milestone clients) on a sample basis and as reviewed also through the BEP on-line system.
    - Provision of MI from the 1st LOD ensuring adherence to GHB policies and procedures in relation to the origination team and on-boarding; reporting and communicating to the ERC as required.
    - Assist in the monitoring of the business assurance teams and origination teams.
    - To undertake Thematic reviews across identified specific business areas.
    - To deliver ad-hoc, bespoke and ongoing training to ensure employees remain competent.
    - To assist in the review the Data Protection (GDPR) framework and assist in ensuring the Data Protection policies and procedures are adhered to.
    - To ensure customer complaints are handled appropriately, and addressed within the time-lines in alignment with the firm's compliance with DISP.
    - Any other duties commensurate with the grade and level of responsibility of this post, for which the post holder has the necessary experience and/or training.
    - Advisory services with respect to product knowledge surrounding HPP, BTL, the end to end operational process, incidents and breaches, audits.
    - 1st LOD QA monitoring review assurance of BDM’s Direct Advise (including Commercial Flows), Specialistr Finance, MK Ops and independent bench testing.
    - Identification of actual and potential compliance risks across the HPP Ops framework.
    - Review of 1st LOD exception cases in comparison with policy & regulatory standards.
    - CMP remedial action points to follow up, track and report progress undertaken by 1st LOD.
    - Assist in the Investigation of incidences/breaches, conduct risk, T & C, Training, SLA’s
    - Deliver training to the business.

Company-wide duties:

  • Demonstrate strong customer focus, both internally and externally.
  • Build and maintain lasting working relationships with fellow employees and any relevant external contacts.
  • Demonstrate the Banks behaviours and values at all times.
  • Support the development of a strong team with high standards of risk awareness and ethical behaviour.
  • Ensure that the Bank is managed safely by understanding and following all Bank policies, procedures and regulations, escalating any issues to line management on a timely basis. This includes notification of all risk events.

In addition to the responsibilities summarised above, you are required to comply at all times with the Conduct Rules set out in the FCA Handbook and the PRA Rulebook. These are listed below. Rules 1 to 5 apply to you by virtue of your employment with Gatehouse Bank and the nature of your role.

Rule 1 You must act with integrity.
Rule 2 You must act with due skill, care and diligence.
Rule 3 You must be open and cooperative with the FCA, the PRA and other regulators.
Rule 4 You must pay due regard to the interests of customers and treat them fairly.
Rule 5 You must observe proper standards of market conduct.

Skills required

Essential Skills:

  • An understanding of Mcobs

Desirable skills, knowledge and experience:

  • An understanding of home purchase plan products or mortgage life cycle
  • Risk awareness of issues identified for escalation, proposing resolutions
  • Regulatory standards awareness

Qualifications

Relevant banking experience in a similar challenger bank role

Training and competence

  • Ensure all regulatory training courses issued internally are completed and passed within required timelines.
  • Being pro-active in identifying learning and development opportunities that would further add to and enhance their skill-set for the role
